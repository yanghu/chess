Lichess study of this chapter

The sudden attack from a different side

Similar to general theory, the idea is to first attacking on one side, then, with greater mobility of the pieces, quickly transfer attack to the other side and break through, before the opponent can respond.

Rooks and pawns endings

The rooks and pawns endings are difficult. Usually, the idea is to forcing opponent to defend a weak point, removing their mobility, and use our own greater mobility to switch side and attack.
